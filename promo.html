FAQs

  1. I am not yet a PAPSI member. Can I still avail of the promo?

    Yes. Non-members may still avail of the promo by completing the registration form. The PAPSI membership fee will be included in the total computation, and your membership will be activated upon release of the training inclusions.

  2. When will the training be conducted?

    All trainings included in this promo are past PAPSI webinars. Once you avail of the promo, you will receive on-demand access to the Zoom recordings and learning modules, which are conveniently accessible through the PAPSI Member Portal. This allows you to complete the trainings at your own pace.

  3. What date will appear on my certificate?

    The certificate dates will start from your date of availment and will be assigned consecutively based on the number of training days included in the selected bundle.

  4. Can I choose the dates that will appear on my certificate?

    Yes. Participants may request their preferred dates to appear on the certificate.

  5. Where and how can I pay?

    Payment instructions will be sent after you complete the registration form. PAPSI accepts QR payments, online transfers, and manual deposits to our BPI bank account.

  6. Do these trainings include CPD units?

    Please note that these trainings do not carry CPD units.

  7. What is the validity period of the PAPSI membership?

    The PAPSI membership is valid for one (1) year from the date of activation.

  8. Can I access the PAPSI Member Portal using a mobile phone?

    Yes. The PAPSI Member Portal is a web-based application and can be accessed using a mobile phone, tablet, laptop, or desktop computer, provided you have an internet connection.
